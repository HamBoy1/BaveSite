BAVID BENNING

Bavid Benning is a man of great power. Though his origins remain unclear, scholars have developed several theories to explain his existence. In addition to this, myths have arisen regarding the events of his early life.

Theories of Origin

Reciprocal Energy theory

The dominant Western theory is that Mr. Benning is a concentration of energy formed in an area with a high student population. This theory, known as the reciprocal energy theory maintains that students emit a nearly undetectable field of various emotions, most commonly stress, sadness, and boredom. Interestingly, this theory maintains that Mr. Benning is not a being made up of these emotions, but a reciprocal response to these negative emotions. Thus, rather than being a creature of concentrated negativity, Mr. Benning is a being of high positive energy. This balance keeps the energy fields around GHS stable. Otherwise, some speculate that the area's saturation of negative energy could cause disaster, including erratic weather patterns and spontaneous increase in the force of gravity.

Community Fiction theory

Another theory states that Bave may in fact be a collective dream or imagining. This is called the community fiction theory. This theory is considered by some to be the most plausable, as most reports of Bave are contradictory. This would support the theory in that, while most general qualities of Mr. Benning are agreed upon, there is room for individual variance, as is typical in a collective fiction. For instance, some have claimed him to be around 8 feet tall, while others say that he has no height and, rather, fills the volume of whatever container he's in. A list of statements regarding Mr. Benning's attributes is included below. 

   List of reported attributes
  • A voice oustide of the human hearing range, but within the hearing range of dogs
  • Skin that changes color and texture on command, giving him the ability to blend in with his surroundings

Guardian theory

The third theory is that Bavid Benning is a guardian of the sacred ground of GHS. The theory states that prior to the founding of GHS, the site was home to a really good pasta shop. Bavid is said to have been the denizen of this restaurant, lending it it's supernaturally high quality. Thousands of years later, when GHS was founded, Bave was still at the site. Few reports exist on his actions at this time, but he is said to have been "moping around" and "making pasta slurping noises." He remained throughout the construction and opening of GHS, and has been a fixture at the school ever since. His face doesn't appear in photographs, so he's not pictured in yearbooks that were made prior to the introduction of digital art. He eventually became the principal in order to have more control over the sacred land, and continues to protect GHS to this day.
